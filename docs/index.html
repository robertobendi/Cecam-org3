About

An institution, not a conference vendor.

CECAM — the Centre Européen de Calcul Atomique et Moléculaire — is an organisation devoted to the promotion of fundamental research on advanced computational methods, and to their application to important problems at the frontier of science and technology.

Founded in 1969, the centre's traditional focus has been atomistic and molecular simulation, applied to the physics and chemistry of condensed matter. Over the last twenty years its methods have extended into materials science, biology and medicinal chemistry.
